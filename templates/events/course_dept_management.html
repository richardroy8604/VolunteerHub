How Student Email Parsing & Auto-Registration Works

When a student signs in with their official @rajagiri.edu Google account, the system automatically executes a zero-touch registration script:

1. Email Extraction E.g. msccs2524@rajagiri.edu → Extracts msccs (Short Code) and 25 (Year) + 24 (Roll).
2. Table Lookup Matches msccs against this Course table → Course: MSc Computer Science (2 Years), Dept: Computer Science.
3. Auto Batch & Semester Computes Batch: MSCCS - 25-27 and calculates current Semester dynamically (e.g. Semester 3).
💡 Adding a course short form here immediately enables instant Google Auth registration for all students of that course.
